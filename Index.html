A Bit About Dan

Dan's journey began in the vibrant Chattanooga area, where he discovered his passion for art. After completing his Bachelor of Fine Arts at the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga, he embarked on an enriching artistic adventure that led him to earn his Master of Fine Arts from the University of Florida.

Now settled in the picturesque landscape of South Florida, Dan finds endless inspiration in the coastal scenery and the rich tapestry of life in the region. Surrounded by the beauty of nature, he explores the depths of his creativity and channels it into his art.

As an enthusiastic educator, Dan shares his expertise and passion for art with students at both New College of Florida and State College of Florida. Through his teaching, he nurtures emerging artists, fostering creativity and innovation in the next generation.
